Bolognese Carbonara Lasagna

spaghetti bolognese

Description

Spaghetti bolognese consists of spaghetti (long strings of pasta) with an Italian rag√π (meat sauce) made with minced beef, bacon and tomatoes, served with Parmesan cheese. Spaghetti bolognese is one of the most popular pasta dishes eaten outside of Italy.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Liquidized tomato's
  • Garlic
  • O1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 medium onions
  • 4 rashers smoked bacon

Directions

  1. Put beef into pan on medium heat and cook until brown
  2. Add tomato's, garlic, olive oil, onions, and bacon
  3. Cook until sauce is thick
  4. boil water in pan until bubbling
  5. Add pasta to water
  6. Cook until pasta is to desired softness
  7. Finally mix together for 3min before serving
  8. Enjoy!
